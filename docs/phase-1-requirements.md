# Phase 1 - Requirement Freeze

## Output types (locked)
1) Bra + panty (existing)
2) Panty + custom prompt (existing)
3) Bra-only model, head-to-waist (new)

## Shared model requirements
- Professional commercial innerwear product photography (catalog context)
- Modest, non-suggestive posing
- No tattoos, no visible blemishes
- Hair must not cover bra straps, armholes, or bottom band

## Bra-only model requirements (new)
- Framing: head-to-waist only (no legs, no lower body)
- Outfit: bra only; no panty, no bottomwear
- Pose: relaxed, confident, neutral stance; direct eye contact
- Crop safety: ensure waistline is visible, but no hips/legs

## Style / background / lighting
- Style: clean, premium, minimal studio aesthetic
- Background: warm creamy beige (#F3F0E9) unless brand spec says otherwise
- Lighting: soft diffused, warm tone, no harsh shadows
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 portrait

## Allowed / blocked terms (prompt policy)
### Allowed
- "head-to-waist"
- "upper-body only"
- "bra-only"
- "professional catalog"
- "minimal premium"

### Blocked
- "panty"
- "bottomwear"
- "full body"
- "legs"
- "thighs"
- "hip"
